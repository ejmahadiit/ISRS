Open-ended answer framework

1. Definition
Meaning in 1-2 sentences.
2. Mechanism
How/why it works.
3. Example
Lecture example/application.
4. Importance
Why it matters + limitation.
Universal starter:
“In remote sensing, this concept is important because it connects the physical interaction between EM radiation and Earth targets with the digital data used for analysis. First..., then..., for example..., therefore...”
25+/30 trick:
Always connect your answer to at least two neighbouring topics. Example: if asked NDVI, mention vegetation reflectance, band ratio, false colour/indices, and classification usefulness.
